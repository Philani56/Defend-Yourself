Phishing Scams

Phishing scams involve fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information like usernames, passwords, and financial details by pretending to be a trustworthy entity. Look out for suspicious emails and links.

Online Fraud

Online fraud encompasses a wide range of fraudulent schemes conducted over the internet. Be cautious about sharing personal details or making payments to unknown entities.

Identity Theft

Identity theft occurs when someone uses your personal information without permission for fraudulent activities. Protect your details by using strong passwords and monitoring your accounts.

Prevention Tips

Protect yourself with these easy-to-follow yet effective steps. Prevention starts with awareness and action.

  • Use Strong, Unique Passwords: Avoid using the same password for multiple accounts. Use a password manager to create and store secure passwords.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of security by enabling two-factor authentication on your accounts.
  • Be Cautious of Suspicious Links: Never click on links from unknown sources. Verify emails and messages before interacting with them.
  • Regularly Update Software: Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against the latest vulnerabilities.
  • Review Privacy Settings: Adjust your social media and app privacy settings to minimize data exposure.
